Chili Con Carne

Description

Satisfy your taste buds with the hearty and flavorful goodness of this Classic Chili con Carne recipe. This beloved Tex-Mex dish combines tender chunks of beef, aromatic spices, and a rich tomato-based sauce for a comforting, spicy kick that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're serving it at a game day gathering or just craving a bowl of chili on a chilly day, this recipe is sure to hit the spot.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef (or cubed beef, if preferred)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced (red or green)
  • 2 cans (14.5 ounces each) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions, sliced jalapeños

Steps

  1. Brown the Beef:

    • In a large, heavy-bottomed pot or Dutch oven, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it apart with a spoon, until browned. If using cubed beef, sear the cubes on all sides until browned. Remove any excess fat.

  2. Sauté Aromatics:

    • Add the diced onion, minced garlic, and diced bell pepper to the spot. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

  3. Add Tomatoes and Spices:

    • Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, chili powder, ground cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.

  4. Simmer and cook:

    • Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the chili simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. For more developed flavors, you can simmer for up to 2 hours, adding a little water if the chili becomes too thick.

  5. Add Kidney Beans:

    • About 15 minutes before serving, stir in the drained and rinsed kidney beans. Allow them to heat through.

  6. Serve:

    • Ladle the hot chili into bowls and top with your favorite garnishes, such as shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions, or sliced jalapeños.

  7. Enjoy:

    • Serve your Classic Chili con Carne with cornbread, tortilla chips, or over rice for a hearty, satisfying meal.
